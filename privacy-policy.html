Privacy Policy for HanziHelper

Last updated: [2024-10-10]

1. Introduction

Welcome to HanziHelper. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we handle your data when you use our Chrome extension.

2. Data Collection and Usage

HanziHelper does not collect, store, or transmit any personal user data. All operations, including Chinese character analysis and information display, are performed locally on your browser and do not involve any external servers or third parties.

3. Permissions

Our extension requires certain permissions to function properly:

  • activeTab: This allows the extension to interact with the current tab when you activate it.
  • scripting: This enables the extension to run scripts on web pages you visit.
  • storage: This is used to store your preferences locally on your device.
  • i18n: This allows for internationalization of the extension.

These permissions are used solely for the functionality of the extension and do not involve data collection or transmission.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy.
